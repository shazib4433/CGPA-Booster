Guidelines to Follow

​Our effort is to make the learning process of students continuous and comprehensible irrespective of the external scenario. In light of the recent events, we have decided to offer the students for facilitating discussion between the Seniors and Juniors, to clear their doubts. Seniors will be available in the whatsApp group to help the students to get their doubts clarified.
Interested candidate who have doubts can ask in the respective WhatsApp Group. Once there doubt is cleared then they will be removed from the group within 5 days of their joining. If you want to ask your query in private. Then you can just drop a text in the group itself that, 'Can any one contact me in dm. I have to ask something' and mentioned your personal detail. Seniors will contact you in dm as soon as possible. We would request you to follow this format while asking your query to seniors. 

 
                            Greeting sir/ma'am 
                            Your question (write your relivent question.)
                            Name, Registration No (ABC, 120187**)
                            Department (Like B-tech in CSE)"
Important note :
  • No abusive language.
  • Only hindi and english language is allowed.
  • Ask college and academics related query.
  • No irrelevant question .
  • No need to call seniors until the permission is granted to you.
  • No spamming in whatsapp group.

    • If someone doesn't follow above guidelines. Strict action will be taken against them.
    His/her doubts will not be cleared and will be immediately removed from the group.
