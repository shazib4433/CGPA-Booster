    Our vision is to create a platform in which we can learn, teach and grow together.
    “CGPA BOOSTER” is an initiative to bridge the academic gap between students and the right study material.

    Our motto is to boost your CGPA by providing you the right study material. All the study materials that has been provided is in direct relation to all the existing subjects. As students, we faced problems during our end terms and mid terms for a quick revision. We are here to remove those difficulties by creating a single platform with all the relevant study material like hand written notes, PPTs, MCQ questions and Live video lectures and doubt clearing sessions. Currently we are providing you the following study material :

      Topicwise PPT Notes
      Chapterwise MCQ questions
      Hand Written Notes
      Practical subject Notes

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Can we trust your study material ?

    Yes, definitely you can trust our study material because the material that we have uploaded are verified by various sources (Teacher / Seniors). The PPTs that we have uploaded is provided by the LPU faculty. So, you can trust and refer it for exam preparation.

    Will this material be sufficient for preparation?

    No, you can't depend 100% on our study material for your exam preparation. This is more like a revision sheet and will help you to boost you CGPA. However, it is advised to attend classes and study regularly. For deeper understanding, do refer to YouTube links as well.

    How is CGPA booster is different than other websites?

    The sources that are available in other platform is outdated because of the change in syllabus over time. So we thought of getting things together with an updated version. Not only are we providing notes but also have planned events such as doubt clearing sessions through various platforms and structured revision classes.

    What is your profit?

    During tough times, like the pandemic, when everything has shifted in the online mode we as students faced a lot of problems to find the best digital content. Our aim is coming up with better solutions for the upcoming students. Therefore, we have kept this completely free of cost.
